About

Twelve kilos. Zero shortcuts.

We roast in batches small enough to taste-test by hand, in a converted garage that still smells like the first bag we ever burned. No blends bigger than our attention span. Every batch gets a roast log, a cupping note, and a number — so when your favourite bag runs out, we can put the exact same cup back in your hands.

Process

From green bean to your grinder.

01

Source

Green beans bought direct from three family farms we visit every season — no brokers, no blind lots.

02

Roast

12kg drum batches, roasted to a logged profile so every bag of a given lot tastes the same.

03

Rest

24–48 hours of degassing before a single bag gets sealed — rushed coffee tastes rushed.

04

Pack

Nitrogen-flushed, one-way valve bags, roast date stamped on every one.
