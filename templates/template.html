About

Small batches, on purpose.

We roast twelve kilos at a time, three days a week, in a converted garage that still smells like the first bag we ever burned. No blends bigger than we can taste-test by hand. Every batch gets a roast log, a cupping note, and a number — so when a café reorders, we can put the exact same cup back in their hands.

Process

Bean to bag, in four stages.

01

Source

Green beans bought direct from three family farms we visit every season — no brokers, no blind lots.

02

Roast

12kg drum batches, roasted to a logged profile so every bag of a given lot tastes the same.

03

Rest

24–48 hours of degassing before a single bag gets sealed — rushed coffee tastes rushed.

04

Pack

Nitrogen-flushed, one-way valve bags, roast date stamped on every one.
